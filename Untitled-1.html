Biography

Chudi Lingareddy is the elected Sarpanch (Village Head) of Thallasingaram, a key village under Nuthankal Mandal in the Suryapet District of Telangana State. A grassroots leader committed to rural development, Lingareddy has emerged as a prominent figure in local governance through his inclusive leadership and dedication to public service. he has a big team name as TEAM CLR
